About

Apollon is made for enlightening the world of masternode coins!

Masternode coins for everybody!

Nowadays masternode coins are not that easy to build an run.

This is mainly caused by two problems:

  • It's pretty difficult to get your own node up and running (especially for beginners).
  • There is no suitable monitoring environment for your running masternodes (independently of the used coin) available.

Just at this moment the Apollon project takes place. Our mission is to make the "world of masternode coins" accessible for anybody as well as providing a holistic toolchain for building and monitoring masternodes on any platform.

More than just a block explorer

Our toolchain is way more than just a block explorer. We are able to track various masternode wallets for different coins out there.

What's the benefit?

With our integrated toolchain you are always able to keep track of all your masternodes at once. Monitor daily rewards, manage alerts if nodes went offline, keep track of coin prices and lots more.

Just take a look at our whitepaper to learn more!
